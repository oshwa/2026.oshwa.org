dr lee wilkins is an artist, cyborg, and technolgoist currently based in Canada. Their work spans digital fabrication, alternative materials, feminist media studies, outer space and open source technologies. lee holds a BFA from Concordia in Computation Arts, an MFA from OCAD University in Digital Futures, and a PhD form the University of Toronto. lee is a board member of OSHWA and executive director of Radio Snack, a community makerspace in Montreal.
