presenter: Eitan Rieger
title: 'Wobble World'
---

Wobble World is an open source gaming console Ive developed from scratch. Originally for the maker space Im a member of (xHain Berlin). The gaming console is based on a 64x64 led matrix and an accelerometer. The control of the games is being done by swaying the entire console - so no buttons or joysticks are being used. The console has a few games programmed but the idea is to increase the selection of games. right new there are:  QTis - 4D tetris game where there is no up and down Snaik - the classic Snake game Poing - A modern take on Pong, where the player controls the ball  instead of the paddles.
