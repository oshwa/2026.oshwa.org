The "Stylish! Belt Buckle Music Synthesizer" is an open-souce virtual analog synthesizer with a stylus based interface built on the Bluepill (STM32F103CBT6), Arduino environment, and Mozzi libraries. Every aspect of the product from code to PCB layouts, 3d printed / CNC / injection molded enclosures, and manuals are all available on Github. There is also a DIY kit version called "Talkie" that is shaped like a little walkie-talkie radio. I'll also have some other fun stuff to show, such as 3d printed enclosures for Hackaday conference badges, stage gear, blinky earrings, music, and more! Support trans creators!
