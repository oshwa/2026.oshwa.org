title: Threadboards!
---

For the past year I've been knitting a series of working breadboards (aka "threadboards") to explore the overlap between textiles and electronics. I developed a knitting pattern for it that wound up being featured in the most recent issue of MAKE Magazine. I'd love to bring the boards to the summit for folks to play around with and make simple soft circuits, and to encourage them to make their own!
