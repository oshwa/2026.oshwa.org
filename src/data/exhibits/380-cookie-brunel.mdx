title: New Travel/Rare Access Point
---

New Travel was an immersive physics puzzle built with open source technology including Arduino and Prusa 3D printers. From this project, I will exhibit an interactive sound sculpture that emits tones at resonant frequencies of PLA. I will also distribute documentation (a zine or art book, tbd) about "Rare Access Point" which was another science fictional installation and performative workshop I made that focused on themes of open source, right to repair, and permacomputing.
