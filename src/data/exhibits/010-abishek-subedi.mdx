title: 'Step Forward: A Mobile TENS/EMS Solution for Gait Rehabilitation'
---

Our TENS/EMS machine is a compact, mobile, and easy-to-use device designed for everyday rehabilitation needs. It is lightweight and handy, so patients can comfortably use it at home or while moving, without depending on bulky clinical equipment. The device primarily targets foot drop patients by stimulating specific muscles and nerves to improve muscle activation and coordination. By supporting controlled muscle contraction, it helps improve gait, balance, and walking confidence over time. The system is designed to be practical, affordable, and user-friendly, making rehabilitation more accessible while still being effective for long-term neuromuscular recovery.
