presenter: Grandy Carson, Dave Zobel
title: 'Ocellight: The Perception Bustier'
---

Inspired by the facial features of your friendly neighborhood spider, Ocellight: The Perception Bustier is an open-source wearable that manifests nearby motion through domes of illuminating ocelli on a symbolically charged female garment. Proximity becomes light, turning personal space into something you can sense — and see.
