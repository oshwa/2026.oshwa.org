title: OSH Vacuum project

The OSH Vacuum project, a joint effort of Open Source Ecology Germany and Intuity Media Lab, showcases three milestones: a benchtop build exposing major functional units for testing and accessibility; design and functional prototypes of housing, cyclone, and more; and a working proof-of-concept vacuum cleaner.
