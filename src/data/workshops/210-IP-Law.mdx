Gather together to discuss **IP Law Meets Open Source Hardware: Navigating Rights, Risks & Opportunities"**.

<LightningTalk title="IP Law Meets Open Source Hardware: Navigating Rights, Risks & Opportunities">
   This session examines key IP issues related to copyright, patents, design rights, and trade secrets, and the differences between software and hardware protection. It highlights legal risks and explores strategies such as copyleft, defensive publication, and licensing to preserve long-term openness.

  <Presenter name="Greta Sparzynski and Tarmio Frei">
        Greta Sparzynski and Tarmio Frei are Research Assistants and doctoral candidates at Bucerius Law School in Hamburg. Their research focuses on intellectual property and technology law, including AI regulation, data protection, games law, and open source hardware. They are part of Fab City Hamburg.
