The Open Footwear project is your path to sustainable fashion. We’ve created an open-source shoe design that you can make yourself and adapt to your needs and aesthetic preferences. Join us to learn how to make your own shoes because: Making is sustainable!

<Presenter name="Open Footwear">
We launched Open Footwear as an independent research project because we refuse to accept the old fashion-footwear model.
Digital tools and manufacturing finally give us the freedom to rethink everything: open-source creativity, local production using nearby resources, designs that adapt to real people — all economically realistic. When production stays close to consumption, repair becomes obvious and products last longer. Local isn’t just better. It’s the sustainable path forward.
