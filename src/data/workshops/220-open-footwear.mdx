We will provide a practical education in a sewing workshop, teaching you how to build your own customised shoes based on open-source designs. 
During the session, we will talk about the background of the Open Footwear project, how it is connected to the Fab Lab community, and our plans for future open hardware community building.
We will bring along some materials so that we can work together to make some Open Barefoot shoe prototypes. 
We encourage runners among the conference participants to join us to discuss potential future running events where we can meet and run together, testing our Open Run shoe or our new model to be sneak-previewed at the summit.

<Presenter name="Open Footwear">
We launched Open Footwear as an independent research project because we refuse to accept the old fashion-footwear model.
Digital tools and manufacturing finally give us the freedom to rethink everything: open-source creativity, local production using nearby resources, designs that adapt to real people — all economically realistic. When production stays close to consumption, repair becomes obvious and products last longer. Local isn’t just better. It’s the sustainable path forward.
