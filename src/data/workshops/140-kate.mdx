Pancake vibration motors have huge haptic potential yet connecting to tiny wires stumps both novice and experienced practitioners. This workshop shares experimental techniques and WIP breakout boards for use with vibe motors. Participants will create “Buzz Bites” - modules with varying styles of electrical connections ready for your next haptics project!
