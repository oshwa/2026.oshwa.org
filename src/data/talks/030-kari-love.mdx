While opera and open source hardware might seem like an unusual combination, for Primero Sueño, performed at The Met - Cloisters, wearable technology was baked into the vision from the start. These wearables not only enhanced the storytelling, but fulfilled key technical needs in the sensitive museum space: sound, light & projection.
