Fantasy Fibre Mill will present videos and yarn samples from our farm scale machines for processing flax, wool, hemp, cotton and cattails from raw material into yarn. We will also discuss the opportunities and obstacles we've encountered doing international open-source co-design between the EU, UK and USA.
