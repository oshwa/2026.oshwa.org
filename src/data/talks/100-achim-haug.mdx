This presentation showcases how AirGradient scaled its open hardware and software to deliver air-quality monitoring globally. With 50,000 OSHW devices sold in 80+ countries, openness accelerates innovation through community-built integrations and transparent schematics, reduces lock-in and ongoing costs, and helps partners and local teams deploy, maintain, and adapt networks faster - turning data into real-world impact.

<Presenter name="Achim Haug">
    Achim is the Founder & CEO of AirGradient, an impact-focused company building open-source air quality monitors and data tools. Based in Thailand, he champions open hardware, open data, and community-led innovation to expand access to reliable air monitoring. His work supports schools, cities, and NGOs worldwide.
